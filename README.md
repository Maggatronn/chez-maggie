# Maggie Hughes - Personal Website

A playful and interactive personal website featuring a friendly jellyfish blob and magical firefly companions.

## ✨ Features

- **Interactive Jellyfish Blob**: A charming blob that pulses and moves with organic jellyfish-like motion
- **Magical Firefly Friends**: Colorful fireflies with distinct personalities:
  - 🎭 **Playful**: Dart around and play tag
  - 🤗 **Friendly**: Orbit and follow the blob
  - 🔍 **Curious**: Cautiously investigate and approach
  - 💙 **Shy**: Maintain comfortable distance
- **Three Modes**:
  - 🏠 **Home**: Blob works in the header area, fireflies roam freely
  - 🌍 **Roam**: Full-page exploration and interaction
  - 😴 **Nest**: Peaceful sleep with gentle wake-up sequences

## 🎮 Interactions

- **Friendship Dynamics**: The blob gently approaches fireflies based on their personalities
- **Wake-up Sequence**: Charming blinking and stretching when leaving nest mode
- **Peaceful Sleep**: Fireflies fade away peacefully when the blob goes to nest
- **Jellyfish Movement**: Pronounced pulsing and organic motion patterns
- **Responsive Design**: Adapts beautifully to different screen sizes

## 🛠 Built With

- **HTML5 Canvas** with p5.js for smooth animations
- **Vanilla JavaScript** for interaction logic
- **CSS3** for responsive design
- **Spring Physics** for natural blob deformation
- **Particle Systems** for firefly behaviors

The website adapts to mobile devices with simplified controls and optimized performance.

## 🎨 Design Philosophy

This website embodies a philosophy of joyful, playful interaction between digital creatures. Rather than predatory chase dynamics, it creates a friendship playground where different personalities coexist and interact naturally.
